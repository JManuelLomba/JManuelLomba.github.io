About Me

My degree is in Manufacturing Engineering and I'm passionate about designing practical, innovative solutions to complex challenges.

My expertise includes CAD modeling, product and structural design, prototyping, and creating manufacturable components. I enjoy bringing ideas from concept to reality—whether it’s designing prototypes, optimizing manufacturing processes, or developing functional parts.

Proficient in SolidWorks, Inventor, and AutoCAD, I combine technical skills with creative problem-solving to deliver high-quality results. I thrive in collaborative environments, leading projects and ensuring designs meet both performance and production requirements.

Beyond engineering, I’m always exploring new technologies, experimenting with 3D printing, and expanding my skill set to stay ahead in the aerospace and manufacturing industries. I’m motivated by the opportunity to tackle challenging projects that make a real impact.
