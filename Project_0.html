Tilt Table - Prototype

Project Overview

Objective

To build a protoype of a 1/3 scale ateration to the Tilt Table that serves as a proof of concept for loading boxes onto the cart with an automated system.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Team Lead
  • CAD designer — full 3D modeling and mechanical planning
  • BOM and Cut list
  • Assembly ordering

Engineering Features

  • Adjustable Tilt Mechanism
  • Locking/Braking System
  • Control Interface
  • Pneumatic Actuation System

Tools & Software

SolidWorks (CAD Modeling), Simulation & Analysis, lathe/Milling, CNC Plamsa, Controls & Automation, and Testing & Mesurements

Outcome

Successfully operated through muiltple tests while maintaining ±1° angular accuracy and reduced manual effort for single-user operation.
